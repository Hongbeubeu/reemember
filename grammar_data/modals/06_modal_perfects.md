# Modal Perfects — Động Từ Khuyết Thiếu Hoàn Thành

> **Cấp độ:** B2 · **Tần suất sử dụng:** Trung bình-cao — cần thiết để nói về suy luận, tiếc nuối và khả năng trong quá khứ.

## Cấu Trúc

| Công thức | Ví dụ |
|-----------|-------|
| `S + modal + have + V3 (past participle)` | She **must have left** early. |
| **Phủ định:** `S + modal + not + have + V3` | He **can't have said** that. |
| **Nghi vấn:** `Modal + S + have + V3?` | **Could** she **have known**? |

> **Tất cả modal perfects** đều dùng cùng cấu trúc: **modal + have + V3**.
> Chú ý phân biệt **V3** (past participle) với V2 (past simple): gone (V3) vs. went (V2).

---

## Tổng Quan Các Modal Perfects

| Modal Perfect | Nghĩa chính | Ví dụ |
|---------------|-------------|-------|
| **must have + V3** | Suy luận chắc chắn về QK | She **must have been** tired — she fell asleep immediately. |
| **can't/couldn't have + V3** | Phủ định suy luận (không thể là...) | He **can't have known** — he was abroad at the time. |
| **should have + V3** | Tiếc nuối / phê bình QK | You **should have called** before coming. |
| **shouldn't have + V3** | Hối tiếc về điều đã làm | I **shouldn't have eaten** so much. |
| **could have + V3** | Khả năng QK không được thực hiện | She **could have passed** but she didn't try hard enough. |
| **might have + V3** | Suy luận yếu / khả năng QK | He **might have taken** a different route. |
| **would have + V3** | Kết quả điều kiện QK (loại 3) | If I had known, I **would have helped**. |
| **needn't have + V3** | Đã làm nhưng không cần thiết | You **needn't have bought** so much food — we have plenty. |

---

## Cách Sử Dụng Chi Tiết

### 1. Suy Luận Về Quá Khứ

| Modal | Mức độ | Ví dụ |
|-------|--------|-------|
| **must have** | ~95% chắc chắn (khẳng định) | The lights were off — they **must have gone** to bed. |
| **can't/couldn't have** | ~95% chắc chắn (phủ định) | She **can't have finished** already — it's only been 10 minutes. |
| **may/might have** | ~40–50% không chắc | He **might have missed** the bus. |
| **could have** | ~30% (một khả năng trong nhiều khả năng) | Someone **could have** accidentally **deleted** the file. |

> **Lưu ý:** Dùng **must have** (không phải **had to**) cho suy luận về QK.
> · ✅ She **must have been** at the party — her coat was there.
> · ❌ She **had to be** at the party... (had to = nghĩa vụ, không phải suy luận)

### 2. Tiếc Nuối / Phê Bình Về Quá Khứ

| Dạng | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|------|-------|-------|
| **should have + V3** | Lẽ ra nên làm nhưng đã không làm | I **should have** **saved** my work. |
| **shouldn't have + V3** | Lẽ ra không nên làm nhưng đã làm | You **shouldn't have** **told** him — it was a secret. |
| **could have + V3** | Có thể đã làm nhưng đã không làm | We **could have** **taken** a taxi. (nhưng đã đi bộ) |
| **needn't have + V3** | Đã làm nhưng hóa ra không cần thiết | She **needn't have** **worried** — everything was fine. |

### 3. Câu Điều Kiện Loại 3 (Would Have)

| Mệnh đề If | Mệnh đề chính | Ví dụ |
|------------|---------------|-------|
| `If + had + V3` | `would have + V3` | If you **had told** me, I **would have helped**. |
| `If + had not + V3` | `wouldn't have + V3` | If she **hadn't left** early, she **would have seen** him. |

> **Mixed Conditionals (kết hợp):**
> · Past cause → present result: If I **had studied** medicine, I **would be** a doctor now.
> · Present state → past result: If I **were** more careful, I **wouldn't have lost** my keys.

---

## Dấu Hiệu Nhận Biết

| Nhóm | Từ / cụm từ gợi ý |
|------|-------------------|
| Suy luận QK | `obviously` · `clearly` · `I'm sure` · `there's no way` · `I bet` |
| Tiếc nuối | `If only I had...` · `I regret...` · `It was a mistake to...` |
| Khả năng QK | `perhaps` · `possibly` · `maybe` · `it's possible that` |
| Điều kiện QK | `If ... had ...` · `had it not been for` · `but for` |

---

## So Sánh Nhanh

| | Must have | Might have | Could have | Should have | Would have |
|-|-----------|-----------|-----------|------------|------------|
| **Loại** | Suy luận (~95%) | Khả năng (~40%) | Khả năng (~30%) / cơ hội bỏ lỡ | Tiếc nuối/phê bình | Điều kiện QK |
| **Phủ định** | can't have | might not have | couldn't have | shouldn't have | wouldn't have |
| **Ví dụ** | He **must have** left. | She **might have** known. | You **could have** called. | I **should have** studied. | We **would have** won. |

---

## Lỗi Thường Gặp

| ❌ Sai | ✅ Đúng | Giải thích |
|-------|---------|-----------|
| She **must had** worked hard. | She **must have** worked hard. | Modal perfect = modal + **have** + V3 |
| He **should had** told me. | He **should have** told me. | **have** (không phải **had**) sau modal |
| They **could of** won. | They **could have** won. | "of" là lỗi phát âm → phải là **have** |
| She **must have** **went**. | She **must have** **gone**. | Sau have → **V3** (gone), không phải V2 (went) |
| If I had known, I **would have help**. | If I had known, I **would have helped**. | would have + **V3** (helped) |
